## üé® Unique Design Elements

### "Business Management with Heart"
Unlike typical management sims, MS Tour humanizes every system:
- Passengers have names, traits, personalities
- Employees are individuals with skills and stories
- Family (wife, children) are deeply integrated
- Historical figures (VIPs) have authentic characterization

### Three-Act Life Story
The narrative spanning 60 years creates:
- Complete character arc (youth ‚Üí patriarch)
- Progressive complexity (learning ‚Üí mastery ‚Üí legacy)
- Emotional investment (building ‚Üí maintaining ‚Üí passing on)
- Historical immersion (1887 ‚Üí 1950s)

### Skill-Based Management Hybrid
Combines:
- Direct piloting (WASD control, moment-to-moment skill)
- Strategic planning (route design, fleet optimization)
- Narrative choices (family, succession, relationships)

### Data-Oriented Design
Technical approach ensures:
- 60 FPS with 50+ ships
- Efficient batch processing
- Scalable to large fleets
- Performance-conscious from design stage
