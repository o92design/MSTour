   ğŸ”„ Route Intertwining - Two Gameplay Lines

   You mentioned goods vs. people shipping, with "intertwining routes being the main mechanic."

   Design Question: What does "intertwining" mean?

   Option A: Shared Infrastructure

     - Same ports serve both passengers and cargo
     - Passenger demand driven by cargo economy (workers need transport to ports)
     - Economic simulation ties them together

   Option B: Route Optimization

     - Can you combine passenger + cargo on same ship?
     - Passenger ferry stops at island to also deliver mail/small cargo?
     - Efficiency bonus for multi-purpose routes?

   Option C: Narrative Intertwining

     - Tourism growth brings cargo demand (tourists need hotels â†’ construction materials)
